CUAPB Police Complaint Archive

CUAPB has spent years using data practices requests to obtain public records about formal police complaints. The public has a right to this information, but the government does nothing to make it publicly available. This archive provides a means for citizens to search an officer's name and see the complaints that have been brought against them. The archive is extensive, but does not include all complaints made against officers. It also does not include the countless complaints that do not make it into the formal system of a government office.

This system is fairly new. Please contact us with any feedback.
